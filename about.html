Interests

I’m fond of Data Science and an R(cpp) enthusiast. I’m co-organizer of the Grenoble R user group.

Current work

I am a PhD student (2016 - 2019) in predictive human genetics in BCM team, Laboratoire TIMC-IMAG, Grenoble, France. My supervisors are Michael Blum (TIMC-IMAG) and Hugues Aschard (Institut Pasteur). My thesis is funded by the LabEx PERSYVAL-Lab.

I am currently working on a comparison of methods for predicting disease based on massive SNP arrays.

I am also trying to contribute to R

  • by submitting my own packages to CRAN (or just GitHub),
  • by contributing to other projects when I can,
  • by asking and answering questions on Stack Overflow,
  • by posting on R-bloggers.
